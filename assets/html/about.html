DANIEL FOREST.

For 10,950 mornings or 30 years, the search for superior ingredients, craftsmanship, and flavor has been the spark that fuels our early rise. It's this passion that inspired two bakers, far from here, to wake up with a shared dream all those years ago: to create a bakery unlike any other. A place for better coffee, weekend indulgences, satisfying lunches, and treats for life’s moments. How? By perfecting the simplest of recipes, trying new things, and presenting them in spaces designed to delight.

This year, we’re marking 30 years since we opened our first bakery on a bustling corner in the heart of another city. Today, we have a few more locations, and our aim is the same to bring better bread to even more people. Whether you’re in Oslo, Berlin, or San Francisco, this is why we wake up, morning after morning, year after year.
